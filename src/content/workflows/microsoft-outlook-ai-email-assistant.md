3. **Configure Node Credentials:** Double-click each node representing Manual Trigger, Sticky Note, Microsoft Outlook, If, Schedule Trigger, Monday Com, Airtable, Markdown, Set, Split In Batches, Merge, Stop And Error and authenticate with your account credentials.
4. **Activate Scenario:** Test the flow manually by clicking **Test step** on the trigger, verify the output, then toggle the workflow to **Active** to start running.
